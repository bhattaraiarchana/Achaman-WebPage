About Achaman Creative Solutions

Achaman Creative Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is an education-development and academic-support organization based in Nepal.

Who we are

Achaman supports teacher training, research, proposal writing, documentation, educational program design and collaborative learning programs for education stakeholders across Nepal.

The organization works with NGOs, INGOs, gaupalikas, municipalities, schools, teachers, researchers and academic communities to strengthen practical education-development initiatives.

Mission

To support stronger education systems through teacher capacity building, research-informed planning, proposal writing, documentation and collaborative learning programs.

Vision

Inclusive, reflective and locally responsive education practices led by capable teachers, institutions and learning communities across Nepal.

What Achaman does

Focused institutional and academic support for education-development work.

Teacher training and capacity building

Design and facilitation of practical training, reflective teaching support and school-level professional development.

Research and documentation

Support for study design, tools, field documentation, reporting and learning documentation for education programs.

Proposal writing and program design

Concept notes, proposals, implementation frameworks and partner-ready education-development documentation.

Who Achaman works with

  • NGOs and INGOs developing education projects and learning interventions.
  • Gaupalikas, municipalities and local education units planning teacher-support activities.
  • Schools, school leaders, teachers and facilitators seeking practical professional development.
  • Researchers, students and education stakeholders needing academic and documentation support.

Why Achaman exists

Education programs often need a bridge between classroom realities, research evidence, proposal requirements and field documentation. Achaman exists to provide that practical bridge while keeping Shikshak Sewa Aayog preparation as one learner-support sub-program—not the organization’s whole identity.
